Prancing Seahorse Water Rafting

Our Mission

At Prancing Seahorse Water Rafting, our mission is to create thrilling and safe river adventures for all. Our dedicated team ensures every experience is memorable, fostering a love for nature and adventure.

History

Prancing Seahorse Water Rafting was founded in 1990 with a passion for adventure and the outdoors. Starting with just two rafts and a small team, we have quickly grown into a leading company known for thrilling, safe and unforgettable river experiences.

Join us and experience the scenic floats laden with adrenaline. Our legacy continues as we expand to new rivers and adventures worldwide.
